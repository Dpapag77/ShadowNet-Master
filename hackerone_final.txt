## 2. Description
The asset listed above currently returns a NoSuchBucket error. This indicates that a CNAME or Alias record in your DNS is pointing to a cloud storage bucket that does not currently exist.

This "Ghost" state allows an external actor to register the bucket name under their own account, effectively taking over the subdomain.
